# Product Context

## Users
Community members who want to discover, organize, and participate in events. Ranges from casual attendees to event organizers.

## Product Purpose
SDAL is a community platform. The events feature allows members to propose, discover, discuss, and RSVP to community events. Events are moderated and build engagement through comments and attendance tracking.

## Brand Tone
Approachable, community-focused, clear. Turkish language. Professional but warm.

## Strategic Principles
- Engagement-first: Surface the most active/popular events
- Clarity: Show what's happening (title, time, location, cover)
- Participation: Easy RSVP and comments
- Moderation: Admin can approve/reject events

## Register
Product (app UI — design serves feature functionality, not brand identity)

## Anti-references
- Generic event list apps
- Cluttered calendars
- Overloaded cards with too many options
